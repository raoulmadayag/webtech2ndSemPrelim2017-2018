Inclusion

It is about race, gender equality, idigents, disability and others that can't speak about their rights to other people that discriminating and being intolerance to them.

The Barangay has a program for PWDs, Indigents and more. Their target is composed of Four Piece, MHDS, Pregnant Mothers, Lactating Mothers, and Interested Participants.

Racism

For Racism, especially for the Native Languages or Igorots, they don’t experience any racism within the area because the people within the area is in harmony. There haven’t been any cases apartheid.

Gender Equality

The Gender Equality, or LGBT Community, they don’t exclude any of them within the area. The only activity that they have are the pageants for gay. But it is performed per Districts not per Barangays.

Indigents

The usual people which they considered “Indigents” are those who are called “Four Piece”. The Meaning of Four Piece is the scholars and those people who are included in the “Pantawid Pamilyang Pilipino Program” which was introduced by Late President Pinoy Aquino. The activities that are involved here are usually under the part of livelihood that includes actual cooking demonstration. The wife of the head captain of barangay is also a part of the livelihood sector which teaches the art craft.

The Barangay encountered 32 indigents person, their philhealth was paid and they received cash from the government. But when the Barangay have the activities like clean up drive they're the one who volunteer first to help and maintain the cleanliness.

PWD

The Barangay Officials stated that there is only one identified under the PWD. The individual stated by the Barangay officials has moved into a different place so as of the moment there is no longer stated under PWD. But the Barangay officials stated that during the time when the PWD is located in the area of ambiong, the individual received a help from the barangay to continue his studies. The PWD's are not under of Barangay's care but instead they always redirect PWDs to the government because barangay officials doesn't receive any subsidy from the government. But Recently, Baguio City urged to create PWD affairs office. THE creation of a Persons with Disabilities Affairs Office (PDAO) in Baguio City was underscored during the city’s celebration of the 2017 National Disability Prevention and Rehabilitation and White Cane Safety Week. Jill Malingan of PDAO La Trinidad, Benguet served as the guest speaker during the culminating activity at SM City Baguio with the theme, “Karapatan at Pribilehiyo ng Maykapansanan, Isakatuparan at Ipaglaban.”
